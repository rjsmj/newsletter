Why San Bernardino?

San Bernardino is one of the most overlooked yet high-potential Opportunity Zone markets in California. A large portion of the city was designated as an Opportunity Zone in OZ 1.0.

Affordability

San Bernardino remains one of the most affordable urban markets in Southern California, making it ideal.

Population Momentum

As Inland Empire cities grow, San Bernardino is experiencing a resurgence of interest from families, entrepreneurs, and creatives.

Underbuilt Assets

There's a clear gap in quality mixed-use, workforce, and small business infrastructure, which LaunchPad addresses.

Connectivity

With access to I-10, I-215, Metrolink, and the new SBX rapid transit system, San Bernardino is a logistics hub.
